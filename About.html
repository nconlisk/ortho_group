Facilites:

Software developed in-house

The group’s strength has been development of novel in-house software and algorithms. These include: FE packages; new constitutive model routines; virtual generation of bone samples with their micro-architecture; and software for evaluation of homogenised material properties.

Commercial packages

  • Finite Element Analysis: Abaqus 6.10
  • Imaging Processing: Amira 5.2.2 and Simpleware 4.1
  • CAD: Rhino 3, Autodesk Inventor and Solidedge
  • Image analysis: CTAn (SkyScan)

Equipment

  • Zwick/Roell materials testing machine
  • X-ray Microtomography (Micro CT) systems - Skyscan 1172
  • Laser vibrometer
  • NI DAQ equipment
  • Ultrasound systems

The group also has access to a wide range of equipment and workshops at The University of Edinburgh
