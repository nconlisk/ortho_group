μFE analysis of bone



This ongoing research activity aims to understand the relationship between bone micro-structure and its linear and nonlinear mechanical behaviour using μCT images of real bone. Our research has shown for the first time that cortical bone becomes more anisotropic with age, which results in increased vulnerability to fracture due to forces in the less frequently loaded directions.
